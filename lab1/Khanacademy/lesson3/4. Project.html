Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Indulge in the creamy goodness of Chicken Alfredo Pasta, a classic Italian-American dish that combines perfectly cooked pasta with a rich and savory Alfredo sauce.

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 300g
Fettuccine pasta 400g
Heavy cream 1 cup
Parmesan cheese 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta al dente.
  2. Sauté chicken until fully cooked.
  3. Prepare Alfredo sauce by combining cream and Parmesan.
  4. Combine pasta, chicken, and sauce. Toss until well-coated.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Vegetarian Stir-Fry

Experience the vibrant flavors of Vegetarian Stir-Fry, a colorful and nutritious dish packed with a variety of fresh vegetables and tofu, stir-fried to perfection.

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Tofu 250g
Broccoli 1 cup
Carrots 1 cup
Bell peppers 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Press and cube tofu.
  2. Sauté tofu until golden brown.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  4. Season with soy sauce and your favorite spices.
  5. Serve over rice and enjoy!
